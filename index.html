About This Project

I created a conceptual smart city called Serenia as part of my DAA course project. My aim was to connect the topics from the DAA syllabus to different parts of a city and show how algorithms can be used in real planning and real systems. I used ideas like shortest path, MST, graph traversal, greedy methods, and dynamic programming to explain how different zones in the city would work.

This project is fully my own work. I referred to online resources and used ChatGPT only for clarification and formatting wherever needed.
