Welcome to Serenia City

Serenia is a conceptual smart city designed for the Design and Analysis of Algorithms course. The city integrates various DAA concepts such as Graphs, Shortest Path, MST, Greedy Methods, Dynamic Programming, and Search Algorithms into real-world use cases.
