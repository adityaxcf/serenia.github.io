Welcome to Serenia

Serenia is a conceptual smart city designed as part of the Design and Analysis of Algorithms course. This project maps real-world city functions to core algorithmic concepts such as Graph Traversal, Shortest Path, Minimum Spanning Tree, Greedy Techniques, and Dynamic Programming, demonstrating how theoretical ideas translate into real-life applications within a modern smart city ecosystem.
