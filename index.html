Welcome to Serenia

Serenia is a conceptual smart city developed as part of the Design and Analysis of Algorithms course. The project demonstrates how various algorithms such as Graph Traversal, Shortest Path, Minimum Spanning Tree, Dynamic Programming, and Greedy Methods play crucial roles in the functioning of a modern smart city.
