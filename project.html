About the Project

For my DAA course project, I designed a small smart city model named Serenia. The goal was to relate different parts of the city with the algorithms we studied in class. This helped me understand how these concepts can be applied to real-world planning.

I divided the city into zones such as residential, commercial, hospital, government offices, tech park, green belt, and transport hub. After deciding the layout, I matched each zone or function with a suitable algorithm like Dijkstra’s, BFS, DFS, MST, greedy method, and dynamic programming.

I used online sources when needed to clarify concepts and used ChatGPT for formatting support, but the layout, sketches, and explanations were prepared by me based on my understanding of the course.
