About the Project

Serenia is a conceptual smart city designed as an individual project for the Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) course. The purpose of this project is to understand how theoretical algorithmic concepts can be applied to real-world systems such as city planning, routing, public services, and utility management.

I began by sketching the structure of the city, deciding on the placement of major zones such as Residential Areas, Commercial Blocks, Government Offices, Mini Hospital, Tech Park, Energy & Industrial Zone, and Green Belt. The goal was to create a layout where algorithmic ideas naturally fit into the city’s functional requirements.

Since I am still learning these concepts, I referred to online study resources and also used ChatGPT as a learning tool to understand how algorithms such as Dijkstra’s, BFS, MST, and Dynamic Programming relate to real-world systems. The final design, structure, explanations, and connections across zones are entirely my own work, supported by these external references for clarity and improvement.

City Design – Layout 1 (Zoning Map)

This layout represents the main zoning structure of Serenia City. It includes:

  • Residential Zones
  • Commercial and Market Areas
  • Government and Administrative Block
  • Mini Hospital and Healthcare Facilities
  • Tech Park and Industrial/Energy Zones
  • Central Lake and Green Belt Area
  • Transport Hub and Connecting Roads

These placements were chosen to maximize connectivity and visualize the mapping of algorithms such as shortest path routing, resource allocation, and network connectivity.

City Design – Layout 2 (Notes & Planning Details)

The second layout includes handwritten notes and a breakdown of land distribution among various zones. It highlights important planning decisions such as:

  • Relative proportions of residential vs. commercial areas
  • Positioning of essential services like hospitals, police, and transport
  • Functional justification of each zone’s location
  • Infrastructure flow including roads, utilities, and green spaces

These notes played a crucial role in refining the layout and establishing logical connections that align with algorithmic concepts explored in the next sections of the project.
