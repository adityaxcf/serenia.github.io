About the Project

For my DAA course project, I designed a small smart city model called Serenia. The main idea was to take the algorithms we study in class and connect them to real city requirements like roads, utilities, emergency movement, services, and overall planning. This helped me understand where each algorithm is actually useful.

I created a basic layout by dividing the city into zones like residential, commercial, government offices, hospital, tech park, transport hub, green belt, and energy areas. After finalizing the zones, I tried to relate each part of the city with a suitable algorithm from the DAA syllabus such as shortest path, MST, BFS/DFS, greedy approach, and dynamic programming.

I referred to online materials whenever I needed clarity and also used ChatGPT only for understanding certain topics better and organising my work. The diagrams, planning, layout decisions, and explanations were done by me based on what I understood from the course.
