Course Overview

Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) is a core subject that teaches how to create efficient solutions for computational problems. The course covers major algorithmic strategies including Greedy Methods, Divide and Conquer, Dynamic Programming, Graph Algorithms, Backtracking, and Complexity Analysis.

Importance of Algorithms in a Smart City

Modern smart cities depend heavily on data, automation, and real-time decision-making. Algorithms play a crucial role in systems such as navigation, public transport, energy distribution, water management, emergency routing, healthcare logistics, and security monitoring.

By studying these algorithms and understanding their real-world applications, we gain insight into how large-scale interconnected systems like cities are managed efficiently. Serenia City is designed to demonstrate these ideas through zoning, routing, connectivity, and resource optimization.
