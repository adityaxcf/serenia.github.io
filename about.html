Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) focuses on efficient problem solving using algorithmic techniques. Concepts such as Greedy Methods, Divide and Conquer, Dynamic Programming, Graph Algorithms, and Complexity Analysis are applied widely in real-world systems.

This project showcases how these algorithms form the backbone of smart city infrastructure — including routing, energy distribution, water networks, hospital resource allocation, and public safety.
