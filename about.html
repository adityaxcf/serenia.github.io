Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) teaches efficient problem-solving through structured techniques such as Greedy Algorithms, Divide & Conquer, Dynamic Programming, Graph Algorithms, and Complexity Analysis. These concepts are essential in designing modern computing systems and intelligent real-world applications.

This project applies DAA principles to the functioning of a Smart City named Serenia, showcasing how algorithms help optimize routing, utilities, energy, water supply, hospital management, and public safety.
